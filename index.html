How to Play


        
        

          
🌍

          

            Welcome, Commander!


            Grand Strategy is a turn-based wargame for 2–4 players (vs AI or hot-seat).
            Choose a map & era, pick your faction, then conquer the world through industry, diplomacy, and force.


            Victory conditions (set before the game):

            🏰 Capture 3 enemy capitals  |  🌍 Control 75% of territories

            💰 Dominate the economy  |  💀 Eliminate all enemies
          

        


        
        

          
🗺️

          

            Maps & Technology Eras


            Maps — Choose your theater of war:

             • World at War (Grid) — Full global campaign (recommended)

             • European / Pacific / Western Hemisphere — Theater-focused grids

             • Classic, tutorial, and regional maps also available


            Unit Eras — Changes every unit's stats and cost:

            🎖️ WWI (1914)  |  ⚔️ WWII (1942)  |  ☢️ Cold War (1970)  |  🛰️ Modern (2020)
          

        


        
        

          
🎖️

          

            Choose Your Faction


            🔵 Atlantic Alliance — Industrial Powerhouse.

             Factories produce +1 unit per turn. Strong economy.


            🔴 Eastern Coalition — Defensive Juggernaut.

             Infantry defend at +1; bonus in home territories.


            🟢 Pacific Union — Rapid Expansion.

             All land units get +1 movement; tanks can blitz 2 territories.


            🟠 Southern Federation — Guerrilla Warfare.

             All units cost 1 IPC less; first casualty ignored when defending.
          

        


        
        

          
🧭

          

            Navigating the Map


            Click — Select a territory; see details in the side panel

            Drag — Pan around the map

            Scroll — Zoom in / out

            F — Fit entire map to screen

            C — Centre view on your capital


            Fog of War — Toggle with 🌫️ in the toolbar. Enemy units in
            unexplored territory are hidden. Move into a region to reveal it.


            Hover any territory for a tooltip showing its IPC value, owner, and all units present.
          

        


        
        

          
🔄

          

            Turn Structure — 6 Phases


            1. Purchase — Spend IPCs to queue units at your factories

            2. Combat Move — Move units into enemy-held territories

            3. Combat — Battles are resolved with animated dice rolls

            4. Non-Combat Move — Reposition remaining units freely

            5. Production — Deploy purchased units at factories

            6. Collect Income — Gain IPCs for every territory you hold


            Press Enter or the → End Phase button to advance.
            The phase progress bar at the top shows where you are in the turn.
          

        


        
        

          
⚔️

          

            Combat System


            Each unit rolls a d6 — score a HIT if the result is ≤ its attack/defense value.
            Dice are animated on screen. Key land units (WWII era):


            Infantry  — Atk 1, Def 2, Cost 4 IPC

            Artillery — Atk 2, Def 2, Cost 5 IPC  (pairs with infantry for +1 atk)

            Tank      — Atk 3, Def 2, Cost 8 IPC  (can blitz empty territories)

            Fighter   — Atk 3, Def 4, Cost 14 IPC

            Bomber    — Atk 4, Def 1, Cost 16 IPC


            Rolling a 1 when Atk/Def ≥ 3 = CRITICAL HIT (counts as 2 hits!)
          

        


        
        

          
⚓

          

            Naval Warfare


            Naval units move through sea zones — the blue tiles on the grid.
            Sea zones form connected chains across oceans; plan your route!


            Submarine  — Atk 2, Def 1, Cost 8  (convoy raider)

            Destroyer  — Atk 2, Def 2, Cost 10 (hunts subs)

            Cruiser    — Atk 3, Def 3, Cost 14 (shore bombardment)

            Carrier    — Atk 1, Def 2, 2 HP, Cost 18  (hosts 2 fighters)

            Battleship — Atk 4, Def 4, 2 HP, Cost 24

            Transport  — Cost 8  (carries 2 land units; no combat)


            Use transports to invade coastal territories from the sea.
          

        


        
        

          
🏭

          

            Economy & Production


            IPCs (Industrial Production Certificates) are your currency.
            Each owned territory produces IPCs equal to its printed value every turn.


            Factories (🏭) are where units are built. You can only place
            units in territories with a factory — and only up to the territory's IPC value
            per turn.


            Capture enemy factories to boost your own production. Bombers can
            strategic bomb enemy factories to reduce their output.


            More territory → more income → bigger army. Snowball carefully!
          

        


        
        

          
🤝

          

            Diplomacy & World Events


            Open the 🤝 Diplomacy panel to send proposals to other factions:

             • Non-Aggression Pact — pause hostilities for several turns

             • Trade Agreement — both sides gain bonus IPCs each turn

             • Alliance — share vision and fight as partners

             • Demand Tribute — force an IPC payment from a weaker rival


            World Events fire automatically during the game — resource
            discoveries, rebellions, natural disasters, and more. Each event grants
            bonuses or penalties and is announced with a banner. Check the
            📋 Battle Log to review past events.
          

        


        
        

          
🕵️

          

            Espionage & Covert Operations


            Open the 🕵️ Espionage panel to run secret operations against enemies:

             • Gather Intel (5 IPCs, 70%) — reveal enemy units in one territory for 3 turns

             • Sabotage Factory (10 IPCs, 55%) — shut down one factory for 1 turn

             • Propaganda (10 IPCs, 60%) — raise enemy war weariness by 20 points

             • Steal Technology (15 IPCs, 40%) — copy 5 research points from enemies

             • Economic Disruption (15 IPCs, 45%) — steal 15% of their treasury

             • Assassinate General (20 IPCs, 35%) — cut enemy attack by 1 for 3 turns

             • Infrastructure Attack (20 IPCs, 40%) — disable two factories for 2 turns each

             • Nuclear Secrets (25 IPCs, 30%) — gain 30% nuclear readiness instantly


            Operations can fail and expose your spy — triggering war!
            The Eastern Coalition has +30% counter-intelligence.
          

        


        
        

          
☢️

          

            Technology & Nuclear Weapons


            Open 🔬 Research to invest IPCs into technologies each turn.
            Six categories, each with a tree of upgrades:

             🛡️ Infantry — Improved Infantry → Elite Training → Assault Tactics

             🔥 Armor — Heavy Tanks → Blitzkrieg Tactics / Rocket Artillery

             ✈️ Air — Radar Network · Long Range Aircraft → Jet Engines / Carrier Superiority

             ⚓ Naval — Advanced Shipyards → Submarine Warfare

             🏭 Economy — Industrialization (+10% income) → War Economy (+15% production)

             ☢️ Special — Espionage Network · Nuclear Program (requires Jet Engines)


            Nuclear Program (40 IPCs) charges readiness over 5 turns. At 100%:

             • Destroys ~80% of units in the target territory

             • Disables any factory there for 5 turns

             • All factions gain +40 war weariness against you — use wisely!
          

        


        
        

          
⌨️

          

            Keyboard Shortcuts & Tips


            Enter / Space — End current phase

            Escape — Clear selection / open menu

            Tab / Shift+Tab — Cycle through your territories

            H — Open this help  |  F — Fit map to screen

            C — Centre on capital  |  B — Build menu

            A — Attack selected territory (combat phase)

            Ctrl+S — Quick save  |  Ctrl+L — Quick load


            Tips: Always buy infantry first — cheapest per IPC.
            Secure a factory early. Use naval transports to open second fronts.
            Watch for diplomacy proposals from the AI!
